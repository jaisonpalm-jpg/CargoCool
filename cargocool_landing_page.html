Built for real trucking conditions.

CargoCool is designed around the realities of road spray, vibration, heat, debris, and long service intervals. It is meant to mount under the body of a box truck or under a semi-trailer without wasting interior cargo space.

Weather sealed

Fully enclosed refrigerator chamber, weather-protected construction, and rear vent routing to reduce direct water intrusion.

Rugged mounting

Frame-mounted bracket strategy with reinforced structure for extensive road travel and repeated vibration cycles.

Dual function design

Dry storage on one side, refrigerated storage on the other, so drivers get utility and cooling in one underbody system.

Core features

The first CargoCool configuration balances refrigeration capacity, secure storage, and serviceability while staying low-profile enough for underbody installation.

01

12V compressor refrigeration

Lockable access doors

Storage door lifts upward with gas struts, while the refrigerator uses a separate insulated access door with locking hardware.

Road-ready construction

Engineered for demanding travel with reinforced framing, weather sealing, service access, and underbody mounting brackets.

Uses external underbody space so drivers and fleets gain refrigerated utility without sacrificing interior cargo area.
